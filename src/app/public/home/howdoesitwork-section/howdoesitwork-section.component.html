How does it work?

If you are a representative of the household

Sign up

Create your account in minutes and access it from any device.

Create your home

Add members and define your family or shared group.

Assign contributions

Define how much each person contributes according to their monthly income.

Control your finances

View expenses, balances, and alerts easily.

If you are a member of the household

Sign up

Create your account with your email or favorite social network.

Join a household

Enter the household ID provided by your representative.

Declare your income

Declare your monthly income to automatically calculate your contribution.

Monitor your contributions

Check your contributions and stay on top of your financial responsibilities.
