About

Mission

Our mission is to facilitate household financial management through a digital tool that distributes shared expenses proportionally to each member's income, promoting equity, transparency, and harmony in living together.

Vision

Our vision is to become the leading shared financial management platform for households in Latin America, recognized for empowering people to make fair, responsible, and collaborative financial decisions.

Why was SplitEasy created?

SplitEasy was born out of the need of many families and cohabitation groups who face difficulties in sharing expenses fairly. The platform addresses a common problem: the lack of financial clarity and equity in households, especially when incomes are unequal. With SplitEasy, we seek to eliminate unnecessary tension and promote a more harmonious and fair environment for all.

Who are we?

SplitEasy is a project developed by ChabeCode, a team of students from the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences (UPC) committed to offering digital solutions that promote equity and well-being in everyday life. Our approach combines technology with social empathy to solve real problems affecting thousands of households.

With SplitEasy, we want to transform the way people manage shared expenses, offering a simple, intuitive, and fair platform that adapts to the needs of each household. We know that financial management can be stressful, especially when incomes vary, and that's why we designed a tool that distributes expenses proportionally and transparently.

At ChabeCode,we believe that a good life starts with good financial organization. And SplitEasy is here to help.
